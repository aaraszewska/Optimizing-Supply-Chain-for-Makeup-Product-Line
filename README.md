💄 Supply Chain Optimization for Uniquexcel Makeup Brand
📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing and optimizing the supply chain operations of Uniquexcel, a fast-growing beauty brand specializing in skincare and makeup products.

As the company expands across multiple cities in the United States, its supply chain has become increasingly complex. This project aims to identify inefficiencies, reduce costs, and improve operational performance using data analysis.

🎯 Business Problem

Despite high product demand, Uniquexcel faces several operational challenges:

Frequent stockouts
Increasing shipping and manufacturing costs
Delivery delays
High defect rates
Customer dissatisfaction

The goal is to identify bottlenecks across the supply chain — from suppliers to transportation — and provide actionable insights.

❓ Key Business Questions
Which SKUs are at risk of stockouts?
Identify products with low inventory and high demand.
Which suppliers or transportation routes cause delays or high costs?
Analyze supplier performance and logistics efficiency.
Are defect rates linked to specific suppliers or lead times?
Detect quality issues and their root causes.
Which products are the most profitable?
Evaluate profitability considering revenue, shipping, and production costs.
Does transportation mode impact shipping cost?
Compare Air, Road, and Rail transport efficiency.
